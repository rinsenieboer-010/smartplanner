Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

justmyplan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What data we collect

  • Your email address (used for login)
  • Tasks, events, and lists you create in the app
  • Messages you send to the AI assistant
  • Photos or files you attach to messages (processed in real-time, not stored)

How we use your data

  • To provide and sync your tasks and calendar across your devices
  • To power the AI assistant (messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Third-party services

  • Supabase — stores your account data and app content securely
  • Anthropic — processes AI assistant messages. See Anthropic's privacy policy

Data retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at rinse.nieboer@gmail.com for any requests.
